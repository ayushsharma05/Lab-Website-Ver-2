A. Month 1 to Month 3 : 20000 MG - 25 hrs/week - Clear Target 10+ learners through conversions
In the next 3 months (90 days) - Educators are supposed to devote 25 hours / week (100 hours / month) in teaching with weekend evening hours mandatorily on a 20000 INR Minimum Guarantee.

The educator should get a minimum of 10 learners through conversions in this 3 months period with greater than 35% Conversion rate to move to 24000 INR Minimum Guarantee Payout from 4th month with 30  hours/ week (120 hours/month ) availability - weekend hours mandatory.

 Here , A performance review will be done monthly and if by end of the Month 2 - the educator has less than 30% conv rate/high cancellations/other issues - then the 3rd Month of Probation should act as a PIP month wherein the educator has to come closer to the goal set , only then will they continue as an educator in Jetlearn from Month 4.

Educators can mark only 4 class cancellations in these 3 months period unless it 's a medical emergency - with appropriate documents provided.

In these 3 months the educators should strive to achieve a Trial conversion rate of 40% and above and work closely with the Jet Learn Teacher Partner Managers and mentors assigned to ensure they meet the goal.


B.What is an MG ??
We offer an MG program to help Educators in their initial days with JetLearn in case they have a slower start than expected on the platform because of which their earnings are not ramping up as fast as desired. We strongly recommend Educators take advantage of our training team to increase their earnings quickly.

 If the calculated pay is higher than MG, then you are paid the actual pay (MG is not an addi tional amount). If your calculated pay is lower than the MG then you are paid the MG amount. MG model is not applicable to educators who are onboarded on the platform as with no requirement of taking trials; Non Trial or Course teachers. Jet Learn will keep allocating and adding learners as per availability for Non Trial teachers.


The necessary condition for a minimum guaranteed payout is making a minimum of 100/ 120 s lots available as per pre-agreed schedules . Your MG eligible amount depends on the number of slots you open with us, in the calendar month, as well as for the next 30 days, each day of the month.

  • In case an educator has scope of improvement in The Month 0, his/her training can extend for 6 weeks as well.
  • If the educator is not able to clear all metrics of RTTS  evaluation in the training month even after 3 attempts ,JetLearn has all rights to dissolve the contract with immediate effect.

  • The Educators In PIP will be automatically moved to actuals pay and will not be able to leverage MG model till they clear the PIP.

  • Educators will have to serve 1 month notice period , the notice period will be paid out on actual number of classes conducted and not the MG

  • At any given time educators will be allowed to mark 6 class cancellations in a month, the 7th class cancellation (with any reason will be penalised at twice the class rate)

  • Educators cannot cancel any class of a newly migrated/converted learner in the first 4 weeks of assignment as it impacts the learner experience.(if this happens the teacher will be liable for a penalty of thrice the class rate )
  • A Pause of a learner is studied in detail and a Root cause analysis is done which includes - audits of classes / live observations/cpr study/Pause PRM/offer alignment/ Course change offering / teacher Change offering and only then a pause happens.

  • The Renewal Incentive will be paid out to the teacher who has taken more than 6 weeks of classes with the learner before Renewal PRM .
  • Please note that the MG program for any month can change and will be communicated in advance via email

  • If a teacher goes live on or after the 15th of the month, then that month and the next    month are considered their first month, and the month after that becomes the second month.
  • The calculation of the minimum guarantee for the first month will be done on a pro-rata basis as per the number of days in the system for that month.
  • Payment will be made at the end of each month and deposited to your Overseas / India account by the 5th of the following month.
